![Pastor em. Mike Pietsch](https://web.archive.org/web/20241015155606im_/https://mikepietsch.thoughtfulworks.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/mikepietsch-profile.jpg)

Mike has been a pastor of the [**Lutheran Church of Australia**](https://web.archive.org/web/20241015155606/https://www.lca.org.au/) for more than 40 years serving as a parish pastor in three congregations, as secondary school chaplain in several government schools, and as a Lutheran university chaplain and co-ordinator in South Australia.

Mike grew up in Melbourne and has shifted back and forth in his adult life between Melbourne and Adelaide (with a bit of Europe and Canada thrown in).

**Mike has completed three tertiary academic degrees:**

- Bachelor of Arts (Melbourne Uni), majoring in psychology and Hebrew and also studying Philosophy, Criminology and Rhetoric
- Bachelor of Theology (Luther Seminary in Adelaide)
- Master of Theology (Melbourne College of Divinity, now University of Divinity)

He is also…

- the husband of the late [Dr Helen Pietsch](https://web.archive.org/web/20241015155606/https://mikepietsch.thoughtfulworks.com.au/helen-pietsch/)
- the father of three children and four grandchildren
- a Christian and pastor of the [Lutheran Church of Australia](https://web.archive.org/web/20241015155606/https://www.lca.org.au/)
- a person who loves to chat and talk and listen to other people’s views and think through them in meaningful, loving and honest ways
- a thinker who questions everything including those things he values such as faith, the church, logic, politics and all other institutions that comprise our society (including the university)
- a person who values our world and tries to care for it holistically and ecologically
- a person who has been privileged to have travelled extensively through many countries across four continents of the world

Mike Pietsch
